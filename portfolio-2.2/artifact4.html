Introduction

SUPU is an imaginary fusion noodle restaurant which was created for a school project. The aim of the project was to design mockups for an application illustrating a 3 step process.

I created these mockups in figma and added simple interactivity such as navigating between screens and button states.

Logo created by Anita Lam

Take A Closer Look

Onboarding screens

Simple and straightforward onboarding and log in screens to set the tone for users. A forget password button is also conveniently located.

Application Screens

One screen was created for each section of the navigation bar found on the bottom. Active pages are indicated by a change in color in the navigation bar.

Checkout process

The three step process I chose to highlight was the checkout process. A progress bar indicates which step you're on at the top of each screen. As per the UX law of feedback, users like to understand where they are in a process and what comes next.
